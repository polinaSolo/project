Our history

We are a students from HIT college. In the website development course we were given an assignment to build our own website on any topic we wanted. We brainstormed for a long time, contemplating various themes, until one topic captured our imagination the most â€“ the Moon. We became fascinated with the mysteries and wonders surrounding the Moon and decided to delve deeper into it. Our journey began with extensive research, spending countless hours exploring the NASA website, immersing ourselves in breathtaking photographs and captivating videos. We were amazed by the vastness and beauty of the lunar landscape, and our excitement grew with each new discovery. Driven by our shared passion, we embarked on the creation of a website dedicated to the Moon. We wanted to share the intriguing facts and captivating stories we had unearthed during our research. Our goal was to create an informative platform where visitors could explore the wonders of the Moon and gain a deeper understanding of Earth's celestial neighbor.

Our goals and mission

Our project had clear goals and a mission that guided us throughout the creation of our Moon-themed website. Our primary objectives were to educate and inspire visitors, fostering a sense of wonder and curiosity about the Moon and its significance in our lives. We wanted to provide a platform where people could delve into the rich history, scientific discoveries, and cultural significance of Earth's celestial companion.
